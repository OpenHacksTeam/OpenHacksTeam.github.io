Is your resume good enough?

What is this?

This is a platform that checks how thought-out your resume is. The program checks for word choice specifically and compares your resume to more ideal examples of a very good resume and ranks your resume out of 100. Ideally, giving you some feedback which you can work on.

What is your motive

That's a nice question. During these unfortunate times, millions of people have lost their jobs and, we wanted to make a program that can help them. Sure, this may not seem like a lot, but, we just wanted to do our best.
